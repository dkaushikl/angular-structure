2-FA

2FA provides an extra layer of security to your account. As you choose, you'll receive an OTP via SMS or authentication ping through Google Authenticator on your smart phone each time you log in.

1.Install App

To use two-factor authentication, you will need to download the SecureAuth mobile app to your smart phone.

2.Scan Code

Open your two-factor authentication app and scan the code with the camera on your phone.

3.Confirm

Enter the password and verification code generated by your two-factor authentication app.
